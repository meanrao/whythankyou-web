Our Story

Built by a family,
for families

Why, Thank You! started with a very relatable problem: too many duplicate gifts, too many awkward "oh, we got that already" moments, and a birthday party aftermath full of things nobody actually needed.

We're a small team of parents who got tired of the chaos that surrounds gift giving for kids. Every holiday, every birthday, the same scramble: group chats asking what the kids want, duplicate gifts showing up, and grandparents buying the toy your child already has.

We built Why, Thank You! because we wanted something simple enough for grandma to use and powerful enough to handle the real complexity of modern family gifting -- different households, different budgets, different stores.

What we believe

Gift giving should feel generous and joyful, not stressful. Parents shouldn't have to field twenty texts asking what size their kid wears. And grandparents should feel confident they're getting something that will actually be used and loved.

We also believe your family's data belongs to your family. We don't sell it, we don't monetize it, and we don't show you ads. The app is free because we want every family to be able to use it.

Where we're headed

We're a small, focused team and we intend to stay that way. We're not building for viral growth or an acquisition exit -- we're building something we want to use ourselves, every birthday and holiday season.
