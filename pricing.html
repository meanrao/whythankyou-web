Common questions

Is it really free? What's the catch?
Really free. We keep it free through optional affiliate commissions -- if a gift buyer clicks a product link and buys something, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to them. That's it. No ads, no upsells, no tracking.
Do gift givers need to create an account?
They need a free account to claim gifts (so we can prevent duplicates and keep records). Viewing a wishlist requires only the share link -- no account needed to browse.
Will it stay free?
The core app will always be free. If we add a premium tier, it will only include new optional features -- we will never move existing free features behind a paywall.
When is Premium coming?
We're still figuring out which features are worth building. If you have ideas, we'd genuinely love to hear from you -- reach out on our contact page.
