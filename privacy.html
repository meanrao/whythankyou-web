Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: we collect only what we need to make the app work. We never sell your data. We never show you ads. Your family's information belongs to your family.

Who we are

Why, Thank You! ("we," "us," or "our") is a gift wishlist application for families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile app and website (whythankyou.com).

What we collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: Your email address and, optionally, a display name you choose. We use Supabase for authentication.
  • Wishlist content: Gift items, links, notes, and child profile details (name, age, sizes, interests) that you enter into the app.
  • Share tokens: When you share a wishlist, we generate a unique token linked to that list. No personal data is embedded in the token itself.
  • Claim records: When a gift is claimed, we store which account claimed it so duplicates are prevented. Claimers remain anonymous to the list owner.
  • Usage information: Basic, anonymized analytics about how features are used. No personal identifiers attached.

How we store your data

All data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database platform. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using TLS. Supabase infrastructure is hosted on AWS in the United States.

We use Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that each user can only access their own data. Shared wishlists are accessible only via the specific share link you create and distribute.

What we never do

  • We never sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We never share your data with advertisers.
  • We never use your wishlist content to target you with ads.
  • We never give data to data brokers.

Third-party services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase -- database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Expo / React Native -- mobile app framework
  • Apple App Store -- app distribution on iOS

Each service has its own privacy policy. We choose services that have strong data protection practices.

Affiliate links

Some links on shared wishlists may be affiliate links. If a gift buyer clicks a link and makes a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to them. This commission does not affect what items appear on a wishlist or how they are displayed.

Children's privacy

Why, Thank You! is designed for parents and family members to use on behalf of children. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13. Child profiles in the app (name, age, sizes) are created and controlled by a parent or guardian.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@whythankyou.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you via the app or email before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the app after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at hello@whythankyou.com or through our contact page.
